Documents

Documentation is an important part of software engineering. Software documentation is written text that accompanies computer software. When developing a product all major steps are documented. These Documents can be used by future researches and people who interesting about the product

  • Project Charter

    Download

    A Project Charter is a statement of the scope, objectives and participants in a project and is a critical document to ensure that everyone involved in the project is aware of its purpose and objectives

    Project Charter was submitted before commencing work on the project. The group met with and presented the project idea and Project Charter to the CDAP Coordinator. The Project Charter was approved by the Coordinator based on her/his evaluation of the project's preliminary feasibility. Then a hard copy of the Project Charter was submitted by the group to the CDAP Team

  • Proposal Document

    Download

    A project proposal consisted of a detailed description of a series of activities aimed at developing "Smart Dentassist"

    The project proposal was submitted to the supervisor. Then, the proposal presentation was carried out by the research group in front of the panel of examiners

  • SRS Document

    Download

    SRS stands for Software Requirements Specification, which is a document that fully describes the expected behavior of a software system. Functional requirements are documented in an SRS, as are non-functional requirements such as performance goals and descriptions of quality attributes

    4 SRS documents, one for each component by each team member, were prepared and submitted to the supervisor. SRS document for the entire system as a whole was also prepared and submitted to the supervisor

    Drafts of the 4 final documents (one for each component by each team member) will be submitted to the supervisor for review. The final document will be prepared following the assessment of these documents
