Welcome To Smart DentAssist

What is Smart DentAssist ?


“Smart Dentassist” is “an Interactive System for Dental Support and Patient Management” which facilitates patient management, treatment simulation, teleconferencing for dentists and dental information knowledge base in the optimal way.

The objective of this research project is to explore the field of dentistry from the perspectives of technology and ICT and to provide the most suitable solution in the form of a software system.

Technology In The Field Of Dentistry


Dentistry can be considered as the most stagnating field of medicine with respect to the advancement of technologies used. After carrying out the background review on this matter, it was evident that the field of dentistry requires a significant amount of technical facilities and support to overcome the risk factors as well as causes of oral diseases.

Brief Overview Of Oral Diseases


Oral diseases such as dental caries and periodontal diseases are the most common type of diseases plaguing a significant percentage of humans regardless of age factor. Oral diseases are a category of diseases which can be easily diagnosed, prevented and cured but unfortunately however widespread and common oral diseases may be, the most frequently neglected and ignored.

It has been discovered that oral diseases could ultimately lead to cardiovascular diseases, cancer, strokes, chronic respiratory diseases and diabetes.

The main reasons for the high prevalence of oral diseases are quite simple and clear; negligence, poor hygiene, poor education, harmful habits and unavailability of dental health facilities.
